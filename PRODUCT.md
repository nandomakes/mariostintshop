# PRODUCT.md — Mario's Tint Shop

## What this is
Marketing/landing site for Mario's Tint Shop, a real auto window-tinting shop in
Murfreesboro, TN (mariostintshop.com). Authorized 3M Platinum Dealer. Services:
car window tinting, PPF, ceramic coating, Tesla services, commercial films.

## Audience
Local drivers in Murfreesboro / Nashville / Middle Tennessee researching tint,
PPF, or ceramic coating. Also Tesla owners and commercial building managers.
They arrive from Google/Maps (5.0 rating, 128+ reviews) and want to see quality
proof and get a quote fast (WhatsApp-first CTA).

## Register
Brand/marketing (design IS the product). Landing page + per-service pages +
interactive tint/PPF visualizer.

## Conversion goal
"Get a Quote" → WhatsApp message to (615) 410-7170.

## Stack
Astro 5 static + Tailwind v3 + vanilla JS islands. No React. Deployed on Vercel,
repo github.com/nandomakes/mariostintshop.
